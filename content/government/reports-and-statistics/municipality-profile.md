# Municipality Profile — DTI CMCI 2024

The **Cities and Municipalities Competitiveness Index (CMCI)** is an annual ranking by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) that measures the competitiveness of Philippine cities and municipalities across five pillars. This profile covers the 2024 results for the **Municipality of Indang, Cavite**.

> **Source:** [DTI CMCI — Indang Profile](https://cmci.dti.gov.ph/lgu-profile.php?lgu=Indang) · Data as of 2024

## Key Takeaways

- Indang is a **First Class Municipality** ranked **149th out of 509** comparable LGUs nationwide.
- **Innovation is the standout pillar** — 15th nationally, with three #1 national rankings in digital government adoption.
- **Resiliency is strong** — comprehensive disaster risk management plans and land use planning rank among the top 3 nationally.
- **Government Efficiency** (428th) and **Economic Dynamism** (231st) represent the largest areas for improvement, particularly in local revenue generation and business cost competitiveness.
